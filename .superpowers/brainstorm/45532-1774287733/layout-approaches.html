Layout Approaches

How should the main content area and right panel be structured?

A

Tabs Above Content

Chat | Changes | Commit tabs sit at the top of the main content area. Right panel (Ticket | Workflow) extends from window top to bottom, independent of the left content tabs.

Pros

  • Clean separation — content tabs and panel tabs are independent
  • Right panel is a peer-level layout element, not nested inside workflow
  • Natural tab bar position (top of content)

Cons

  • Two tab bars on screen (content + panel) could feel busy
B

Tabs Integrated Into Chat Footer

Instead of a separate tab bar, Chat | Changes | Commit are small pill-style tabs embedded in the bottom bar alongside the workflow indicator. Content area switches without a top bar.
